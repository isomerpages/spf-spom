<h3><strong>SPOM Medal 2017 (Golf)</strong></h3>
<p>24 March 2017</p>

<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="golf 2017" src="/images/golf_2017.png">
</div>
<p>The 12th SPOM Golf Medal, which was co-organised by SPOM Sport Sub-Committee
and Police Golfing Society, saw a total of 34 SPOM members competing at
the 18-hole course at Seletar Country Club (SCC) on 24 Mar 2017.</p>
